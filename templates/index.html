      Resource Efficiency

      Sending 1 kg of cargo to Mars can cost up to $60,000, so recycling the crews 12,600 kg of waste avoids billions in transport costs.

      Sustainability for Future Missions

      On long Mars missions, recycling can cut 3040% of storage and resupply needs, ensuring astronauts long-term survival and self-sufficiency.

      The Project
      It is a completely self-sustaining machine which generates energy using peizocrystals.
      • Separation Process –The machine uses sensors and automated sorting to divide waste into organic matter and plastic materials (like food packaging).
      • Organic Conversion –Organic waste is placed into high-pressure, high-temperature chambers, where it undergoes accelerated decomposition, producing nutrient-rich manure suitable for reuse.
      • Plastic Treatment –Plastics are compressed and processed into thin, durable sheets, which can be repurposed for structural or protective uses.

          F.A.Q

          • All

          Can the machine get hacked?

          The risk of these machines getting hacked is minimal. This is because we apply strong security measures, like regular updates and robust access controls. There would be multiple servers. We also keep them isolated from other parts of the network and use encryption for added protection. In addition, we continuously watch for anything unusual to prevent issues from happening.

          Could animals get stuck inside the machine while collecting trash?

          No, our AI-powered trash collection machine is designed with multiple safety features to minimize the risk of animals getting stuck. It incorporates sensors and obstacle detection systems to identify objects in its path, which allows it to stop or change course if it encounters anything other than trash. This ensures the safety of animals and prevents them from becoming trapped in the machine during the waste collection process.

          How much would the project cost?

          The whole project will be around 8-10 million dollars. This price is inclusive of the EcoSorter 3000 and the secondary machines. It will all be eco-friendly and the money includes the launching of satellites.

          Can the machine malfunction?

          The machine is equipped with safety measures and backup systems to ensure a response, in case of any malfunctions. Trained technicians perform maintenance to minimize the chances of encountering problems. If there is a malfunction the machine can be remotely. A technician will be promptly dispatched to carry out repairs and ensure efficient trash collection.
